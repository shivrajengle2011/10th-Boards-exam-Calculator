Frequently Asked Questions

How is CBSE percentage calculated?

CBSE percentage is calculated using Best of 5 subjects. The highest five marks are added and divided by 500.

How is SSC percentage calculated?

SSC percentage is calculated by adding all six subjects and dividing by 600.
